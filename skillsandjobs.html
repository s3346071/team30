Skills and Jobs

Remote Guided Maintenance Team - Augmented Reality Application

Team 30 is searching for a team of experienced and qualified members to build our Augmented Reality Guided Maintenance software application.
In order for the RGM to be taken to the next stage, Team 30 will require this small team full of people in the IT and motor industry combined.

  • Project Manager
  • AR/Unity Developer
  • Qualified Heavy Automotive Mechanic
  • Full Stack Developer

Project Manager

Team 30 requires a candidate that will be the mastermind behind ensuring the group working on the project is maintaining their duties according
to set deadlines and budgets. As project manager you will also be required to to envision how the application will transform from an initial idea
to a program that is marketable to an audience of potential business investors or sponsors.

Skills Required:

  • Preparation of a strategy to provide to developers for the approach of programming the Hololens and RGM Application
  • Staff recruitment, training and leading.
  • Working alongside team to incorporate ideas for additional improvements or updates that could be required to advance to
    further capabilities in the future.
  • Leading team to success by having a full understanding of the market that is being sold to and any competition you are facing.
  • Customer service skills and communication
  • Experience with all relevant coding languages

Experience Required:

  • 3-5 Years of experience in Project Management
  • Bachelor in IT or Computer Science
  • 5+ Years experience in IT
  • Outstanding communication and writing skills

AR/Unity Developer

Our ideal candidate will be required to successfully contribute with the team to design and create high-quality, formal and
professional Augmented Reality software to present to our client for a new and innovative idea for Remote Guided Maintenance
via the use of the Microsoft Hololens. The AR developer will work closely with the vehicle mechanic to correctly install
labels, interactive software etc based throughout the trucks that are being assessed.

Skills Required:

  • Working alongside mechanics to gain further understanding of truck layouts.
  • Strong familiarity of the Hololens and Augmented Reality software (eg: Unity, Tensorflow, Python.)
  • Collaborate closely with Full Stack Developer to implement creations upon the Application software and UI
  • Practice of coding languages (C#, C++, Java)
  • Strong teamwork skills

Experience Required:

  • Bachelor in IT or Computer Science
  • 2+ Years of experience with C#, C++ and Java languages
  • 2+ Years of experience in Unity Development
  • Experience in VR Development

Qualified Heavy Automotive Mechanic

Candidate will be able to ensure the most efficiency for service between the team of staff and the customer by
having the greatest understanding of vehicles. Therefore, covering all bases of information such as names and locations
of important hardware within the trucks that are being assessed for repair. The candidate will also require the confidence
of being able to decide what will need to be performed to repair the vehicles, upon findings through the Hololens.

Skills Required:

  • Problem-solving
  • Customer service and communication
  • Testing and diagnosing
  • Trade skills (Thorough use of tools and other hardware
  • Detailed inspections of trucks
  • High quality workl standard and meet deadlines.

Experience Required:

  • Certificate III in Heavy Commercial Vehicle Mechanical Technology
  • Minimum of 3-4 years of qualified mechanic experience
  • Driver's license (Heavy Vehicle)

Full Stack Developer

The full stack developer will be responsible for creating the application that is used for all communication between clients
and the staff through the Microsoft Hololens. The Remote Guided Maintenance application is the deliverable for the overall project
and will need to be running at optimal performance at all times to allow the smoothest workflow for all involved in the process.

Skills Required:

  • Soft skills
  • Construction of responsice interface that performs across multiple devices
  • Server architecture
  • Data storage - Use of cloud and local services
  • Proficiency with version control (GitHub)
  • Some understanding of Unity (preferred)

Experience Required:

  • Bachelor in Computer Science
  • Minimum 3 years of experience as Full Stack Developer
  • HTML, CSS, Java, C#
  • Experience with backend languages such as Python, Ruby and PHP
