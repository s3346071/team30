Motivation

Team 30 chose this project for several reasons. Augmented Reality is something that few of us understood, but through our research for assignment 2 and our coursework, we have become excited by its potential. Once only the domain of science fiction, AR is suddenly becoming a real thing and it is claimed that the AR industry could be worth $25 billion by 2025 (Islam, A 2018). So, this is an opportunity to immerse ourselves in one of the cutting edges of IT.

Furthermore, as a useful tool we believe that the RGM has value. The completion of accurate pre-start checks and improving the speed of truck repairs are genuine problems for the transport industry. When mature, the RGM has scope for use in all kinds of machinery maintenance.

Team 30 believe that embracing this challenge to deliver a solution of practical value using a cutting-edge technology would demonstrate innovative thinking and a can-do attitude. These were key personal attributes that came out in our interview with an IT industry professional in assignment 2 as attractive to employers.
