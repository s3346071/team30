Risks

In looking at key risks to the project we needed to differentiate risks from issues which actually arise in a project and exist today (Expert Program Management).

We also had to make some assumptions such as that our scope is clearly defined. This is critical as once established it will allow us to allocate tasks more efficiently and provide direction (Jamaledine, R 2017).

We will follow an agile delivery approach such as scrum (Scrum.org) as part of our broader Agile methodology which will help mitigate many common risks, such as:

  • Scope creep and “gold plating”
  • Inaccurate cost estimates
  • Project team misunderstanding requirements
  • Poor design

Some key project risks that need to be addressed:

Description Likely Impact Mitigations
Users don’t see value in the product Low High
  • Market research
  • Deliver an MVP as early as possible
Lack of skills in developing AR software High High
  • Skill up current project team members; or
  • Hire externally
Hardware supply-chain issues due to Covid-19 High High
  • Proactively manage customer delivery expectations
User confusion over what it can do Medium Medium
  • Focus on usability
  • Offer training and clarity on features covered
